# Privacy Policy — Product Listing Builder

**Last updated:** 2024
**Publisher:** KART L

---

## Summary

Product Listing Builder is a local-first Windows desktop application. Listings, templates, settings, and aggregate counters all live in your browser's localStorage on your device.

---

## Data Collection

Product Listing Builder **does not collect** any personal data. Specifically:

- No usage analytics or telemetry
- No crash reporting sent to external servers
- No account or sign-in required
- No advertising identifiers used
- No third-party tracking SDKs included

---

## Listing Data

- Listings you type into the editor stay in your browser's localStorage
- The per-platform preview is a pure local transformation — no marketplace is contacted
- CSV bulk import reads the text you paste; the file you pasted from is never uploaded
- Export TXT / JSON downloads happen entirely client-side

---

## Local Storage

Product Listing Builder uses browser localStorage to save:

- App settings (theme, default currency, default platform, default condition, business name)
- All saved listings (with every field you filled in)
- Custom templates you created
- Aggregate counters (listings created, duplicated, templates saved, bulk runs)
- First-use date

All of this data is stored **locally on your device only**. It is never transmitted.

You can delete all locally stored data at any time from **Settings → Local Data → Clear all local data**. This does not delete TXT or JSON exports you have already downloaded.

---

## Future Marketplace Integrations

A future paid build may add direct posting to Amazon SP-API, eBay Inventory API, Etsy Open API, Shopify Admin GraphQL, or WooCommerce REST. Those features require seller credentials and OAuth consent, and this policy will be updated **before** that version is released, with users informed clearly within the app.
