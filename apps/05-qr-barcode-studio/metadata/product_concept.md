# Product Concept — QR Barcode Studio

## Problem it solves
Generating QR codes and 1D barcodes is a routine task — for posters, business cards, packaging,
inventory, Wi-Fi sharing, contact sharing, link campaigns — but most tools live on the web and want
to upload your payload to a server. QR Barcode Studio is a local Windows desktop tool that produces
real QR codes and 1D barcodes with a live preview and PNG / SVG export, without ever leaving the device.

## Target users
- Small business owners labelling products, posters, or menus
- Marketing teams generating QR for short campaigns
- Office staff sharing Wi-Fi, vCards, or meeting links via QR
- Warehouse / inventory teams needing quick Code 128 labels
- Anyone who'd rather not paste sensitive data into a web tool

## Core value loop
1. Pick a generator (QR or one of five 1D barcode formats) — or use Batch
2. Type your payload — preview updates live
3. Tune colors, size, error level, or bar dimensions
4. Export as PNG or SVG, or Save to local history
5. Re-open and re-export from History anytime

## Differentiators
- Five QR payload types with proper encoders (URL, Text, Wi-Fi, vCard, Email)
- Five 1D barcode formats with per-format validation messages
- Real PNG and SVG export — vector files included
- Batch mode — paste a list, get one PNG per line
- Local history with thumbnail preview, search, and re-edit
- Privacy-first: no upload, no account, no tracking
- One-time price model (free at launch)
