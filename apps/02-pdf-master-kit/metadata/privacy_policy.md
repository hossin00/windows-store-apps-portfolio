# Privacy Policy — PDF Master Kit

**Last updated:** 2024
**Publisher:** KART L

---

## Summary

PDF Master Kit is a local-first Windows desktop application. Your PDF files and any operations you perform on them stay on your device.

---

## Data Collection

PDF Master Kit **does not collect** any personal data. Specifically:

- No usage analytics or telemetry
- No crash reporting sent to external servers
- No account or sign-in required
- No advertising identifiers used
- No third-party tracking SDKs included

---

## File Handling

PDF files you import into PDF Master Kit are processed within the application on your local device.

- PDFs are **not uploaded** to any server
- File contents are parsed and rewritten in-memory using the open-source pdf-lib library
- Output PDFs are produced as browser downloads to your local Downloads folder
- No copies of your files are retained beyond your explicit save actions

---

## Local Storage

PDF Master Kit uses browser localStorage to save:

- Operation history (if you have enabled "Save operation history locally")
- App settings (theme, filename prefix, privacy mode)
- Aggregate counters (number of operations run, pages processed)

All of this data is stored **locally on your device only**. It is never transmitted.

You can delete all locally stored data at any time from **Settings → Local Data → Clear all local data**.

---

## Future Online Features

If any future version of PDF Master Kit adds online or cloud features, this privacy policy will be updated **before** that version is released, and users will be clearly informed within the app.
