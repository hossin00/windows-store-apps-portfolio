# Privacy Policy — Invoice PDF Builder

**Last updated:** 2024
**Publisher:** KART L

---

## Summary

Invoice PDF Builder is a local-first Windows desktop application. Invoice data, business profile, and PDFs you generate all stay on your device.

---

## Data Collection

Invoice PDF Builder **does not collect** any personal data. Specifically:

- No usage analytics or telemetry
- No crash reporting sent to external servers
- No account or sign-in required
- No advertising identifiers used
- No third-party tracking SDKs included

---

## Invoice Data Handling

Invoice data (your business profile, your clients, your line items, totals, notes) is processed within the application on your local device.

- Invoice fields you type are held in memory while you edit
- When you click Save draft, the invoice is written to your browser's localStorage (on your device)
- PDF exports are generated entirely in-browser using the open-source pdf-lib library
- Nothing is uploaded to any server

---

## Local Storage

Invoice PDF Builder uses browser localStorage to save:

- App settings (theme, default currency, default tax rate, invoice number prefix and sequence)
- Default business profile (name, address, email, logo emoji)
- Saved invoice history (full invoice records you have explicitly saved as drafts)
- Aggregate counters (invoices saved, PDFs exported, line items created)

All of this data is stored **locally on your device only**. It is never transmitted.

You can delete all locally stored data at any time from **Settings → Local Data → Clear all local data**. This does not delete PDF files you have already downloaded.

---

## Future Online Features

If any future version of Invoice PDF Builder adds online or cloud features (cloud sync, email delivery, payment links), this privacy policy will be updated **before** that version is released, and users will be clearly informed within the app.
