# Product Concept — Invoice PDF Builder

## Problem it solves
Freelancers, consultants, and small business owners need to produce clean, professional invoices on demand.
Web-based invoicing tools want a monthly subscription and require uploading client data to a third-party
server. Spreadsheets are tedious and easy to miscalculate. Invoice PDF Builder is a local Windows desktop
app that produces a real PDF in seconds — no account, no subscription, no upload.

## Target users
- Freelancers (designers, developers, writers, translators)
- Independent consultants
- Small business owners doing their own invoicing
- Side-project sellers needing occasional invoices
- Anyone replacing a Word doc / Excel sheet workflow

## Core value loop
1. Set your business profile once in Settings
2. Click New Invoice — your business pre-fills, the number auto-increments
3. Fill in the client, line items, tax and discount
4. Watch the live preview match your inputs in real time
5. Save draft and Export PDF — a real A4 PDF is downloaded

## Differentiators
- Real PDF export with pdf-lib (not a print-screen, not a screenshot)
- Live HTML preview as you type, sharing the layout of the exported PDF
- Auto-increment invoice number with editable prefix and sequence
- Multi-currency (USD, EUR, GBP, MAD)
- Accurate calculations to 2 decimal places — subtotal, discount %, tax %, grand total
- Save drafts, duplicate, re-edit, delete — all local
- Privacy-first: no account, no upload, no tracking
- One-time price model (free at launch)
