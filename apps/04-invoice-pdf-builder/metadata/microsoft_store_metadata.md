## Full Description

Invoice PDF Builder is a Windows desktop utility for creating professional invoices in seconds.

Fill in your business info, your client info, line items, tax rate and discount — see the invoice render live as you type — then export a real, A4 PDF generated locally with pdf-lib. Save drafts, duplicate invoices, re-edit anytime.

**Features:**
- Live invoice preview that mirrors the PDF layout
- Real PDF export with pdf-lib (no print-screen)
- Auto-increment invoice number (custom prefix, custom next number)
- Issue date + due date pickers
- Line item table with quantity, unit price, line total
- Add, remove, reorder line items
- Subtotal · tax % · discount % · grand total — accurate to 2dp
- Multi-currency: USD, EUR, GBP, MAD
- Notes / payment terms field
- Save drafts, duplicate, re-edit, delete
- Local invoice history with search
- Default business profile in Settings
- Dark, light, and system themes
- Privacy-first: no ads, no tracking, no account required

Invoice PDF Builder runs every step locally. No account, no upload, no subscription.
