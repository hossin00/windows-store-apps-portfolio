# Privacy Policy — PDF OCR Desk

**Last updated:** 2024
**Publisher:** KART L

---

## Summary

PDF OCR Desk is a local-first Windows desktop application. Your files and data stay on your device.

---

## Data Collection

PDF OCR Desk **does not collect** any personal data. Specifically:

- No usage analytics or telemetry
- No crash reporting sent to external servers
- No account or sign-in required
- No advertising identifiers used
- No third-party tracking SDKs included

---

## File Handling

Files you import into PDF OCR Desk are processed within the application on your local device.

- Imported images and PDF files are **not uploaded** to any server
- File contents are processed in-memory and optionally saved to your browser's localStorage
- No copies of your files are retained beyond your explicit save actions

---

## Local Storage

PDF OCR Desk uses browser localStorage to save:

- OCR history (if you have enabled "Save OCR history locally")
- App settings (theme, language preference, export format)
- Export records log

All of this data is stored **locally on your device only**. It is never transmitted.

You can delete all locally stored data at any time from **Settings → Local Data → Clear all local data**.

---

## Future Online Features

If any future version of PDF OCR Desk adds online or cloud features, this privacy policy will be updated **before** that version is released, and users will be clearly informed within the app.

---

## Contact

For privacy questions or concerns, contact KART L via the Microsoft Store app listing support channel.
